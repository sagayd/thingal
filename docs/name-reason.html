Why the Name “Thingal Illam”?

Thingal means Moon in Tamil.

Thingal = Thinmaiyaana (Very strong) + Gal (stone)

The Blocks used for constructing are made us using CSEB. It is very strong stone. Hence as Thingal as an alias.

Illam = ILL (house) + AM (beatiful) => Beautify house

Illam = Beautiful house made with very stong (mud) blocks
